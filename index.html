Our Heritage

Founded in Amman in 1998, Kanaan Fermès de Dattes cultivates exceptional Medjool dates rooted in Levantine tradition. What began as a family agricultural enterprise has evolved into a refined export brand serving Europe and the East Coast of the United States.

Our dates are hand-selected for velvet texture, caramel sweetness, and a deep honeyed complexity that reflects the sun-drenched groves of Jordan.

The Crown Jewel of the Levant

In the heart of the Jordan Valley, where the sun sits heavy over the earth and the soil holds the secrets of millennia, grows the Medjool date—rightfully known as the "Fruit of Kings." For the people of Amman and the wider Levant, the date palm is more than agriculture; it is a witness to history. Since 1998, Kanaan Fermès de Dattes has been the steward of this ancient lineage. What began as a humble family devotion in Amman has blossomed into a bridge between cultures, carrying the soul of Jordan to dinner tables in Europe and the busy streets of the American East Coast.

More than a Harvest: A Cultural Pulse

In Eastern civilization, the date palm is the "Tree of Life." To offer a date is to offer hospitality; to share one is to create a bond. In the bustling markets of Amman, these dates represent the rhythm of the seasons and the endurance of our heritage.

Global Reach

From the markets of Amman to luxury boutiques across Europe and select estates along the American East Coast, Kanaan Fermès de Dattes delivers a sensory experience defined by quality and refinement.

The German Gateway: Precision Under Pressure

In the demanding markets of Berlin, Hannover, and Munich, the Medjool date is revered as a pinnacle of natural luxury. To meet the rigorous standards of the German palate, we revolutionized our infrastructure. Recognizing the volatility of Central European climates, we developed specialized industrial packaging designed to act as a thermal barrier. This proprietary layering ensures that the honeyed moisture of the fruit remains intact, even when transitioning from the arid warmth of the Jordan Valley to the sub-zero temperatures of a German winter. By neutralizing thermal shock, we guarantee that a Kanaan date in Munich tastes identical to one plucked hours ago in Amman.

The Mediterranean Presence: Spain and France

Our entry into the cultural hubs of Madrid and Barcelona was a homecoming of sorts—reintroducing the Medjool to a region that deeply respects the confluence of Moorish history and modern gastronomy. In Spain, our dates have found their way into high-end culinary circles, celebrated for their ability to pair with both aged cheeses and avant-garde desserts.

Similarly, in France, we have positioned ourselves within the épiceries fines of Paris and Lyon. Here, our distribution channels are defined by "Just-in-Time" delivery models. By utilizing sophisticated, climate-controlled hubs, we minimize the time between harvest and shelf. This ensures that the delicate skin of the Medjool—which can be compromised by improper handling—remains pristine and unblemished for the most discerning connoisseurs in the world.

The Transatlantic Alliance: Bridging Continents

Our expansion into the United States was not merely a logistical feat, but a strategic synergy. Through a landmark collaboration with Abu Ayyash Farms—the pioneering force in Medjool cultivation and global export—we successfully introduced the Kanaan signature to the most discerning markets in North America.

From the Jordan Valley to the American Seaboard

By aligning our heritage with the established expertise of Abu Ayyash Farms, we unlocked the complex gateway to the American East Coast. This partnership allowed us to navigate the rigorous quality standards of U.S. agricultural imports while maintaining the delicate integrity of our fruit. Today, our dates are a staple of refinement in two of the most vibrant regions in the country:

  • New York: The Epicenter of Gastronomy In the high-end artisanal markets of Manhattan and the specialized boutiques of Brooklyn, Kanaan dates are celebrated as a natural luxury. We have integrated into the city’s fast-paced, health-conscious culinary scene, providing a sophisticated alternative to processed sweets.
  • Florida: A Climate of Shared Sunlight From Miami to Palm Beach, our dates have found a natural home in the Sunshine State. The tropical affinity for high-quality fruit makes Florida a key hub for our distribution, where the "honeyed complexity" of a Jordan Valley Medjool is recognized as the gold standard of the category.

This collaboration is built on a shared philosophy: that the Medjool date is a gift of the earth that deserves a global stage. By leveraging the pioneering infrastructure of Abu Ayyash Farms, we have streamlined our supply chain to ensure that the journey across the Atlantic does not diminish the "velvet" texture our customers expect. It is a testament to what can be achieved when two guardians of Levantine tradition join forces to serve the world.

Personal Profile

Sanad Kanaan

A profile shaped by heritage, discipline, leadership, and a long-term vision for business, strategy, and global impact.

From the Heart of Jordan

My story begins in Jordan, where I was born and raised. Growing up in a culture that deeply values heritage and hospitality, I learned early on that a business is never just about transactions—it is about the people it serves and the legacy it leaves behind. This family business is my heartbeat. I hold a deep-rooted commitment to leading this family into its next chapter, ensuring that our values remain the foundation of everything we build.

The Intersection of Strategy and Precision

I have always been fascinated by how things work—not just the mechanics, but the math behind the success. This curiosity led me to Gonzaga University, where I am currently pursuing a double major in Engineering Management and Finance.

I believe the future belongs to those who can bridge the gap between technical execution and financial strategy. My long-term vision is to operate at the intersection of the banking and consulting worlds, fusing the analytical discipline of an engineer with the strategic foresight of a financial expert to solve complex, global challenges.

Professional Foundations

Before stepping into my current leadership role, I sharpened my professional edge at Hikma Pharmaceuticals, one of the most prestigious pharmaceutical companies in the MENA region. Working within such a high-stakes, highly regulated industry taught me the importance of precision, operational excellence, and the impact that a well-run organization can have on a regional scale.
